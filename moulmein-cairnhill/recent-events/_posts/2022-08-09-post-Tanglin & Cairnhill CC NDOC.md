<p>MoCa is unique in that we have 3 community clubs in our division. So after
Pek Kio Community Centre’s National Day Celebrations this morning, we proceeded
to Tanglin Community Club for a joint NDOC with Cairnhill Community Club!</p>
<p>Happy National Day and a big thank you to all our volunteers who put this
together today at our iconic Tanglin CC!</p>
