<p>Our Annual MoCa Easter Eggstravaganza is a spectacular tradition for families!
Put together lovingly by our Cairnhill NC and Cairnhill Community Club,
it's an amazing afternoon of magic, bunnies, craft, games and more for
all ages!</p>
<p>So EggCited to bring this our community every year! It's a great way to
yolk and bond</p>
