<p>Visited friends from VOGX and Lily Tan Mansion on a cool Sunday afternoon!</p>
<p>KC and Rose were wonderful hosts at VOGX who are stalwarts of our talented
MoCa Ukelele Band, while Kenny and the Goh family shared how they were
long time People's Action Party supporters and activists, with the likes
of Mr LKY and Mr Toh Chin Chye.</p>
<p>Thanks Cambridge Neighbourhood Committee for your wonderful support this
afternoon helping us distribute national flags to our residents!</p>
