<p>Took our MoCa residents to visit the amazing “LKY: The Experience” exhibition
at Prinsep Link today.</p>
<p>As the young creators Kexin and Wei Ting described, calling it an exhibition
doesn't quite do it justice. It was a moving experience of Mr Lee Kuan
Yew's incredible life rendered in engaging interactions in the many rooms
that make up the “experience”.</p>
<p>Having just re-read “The Singapore Story”, “LKY: The Experience” had even
more familiarity and meaning for me.</p>
