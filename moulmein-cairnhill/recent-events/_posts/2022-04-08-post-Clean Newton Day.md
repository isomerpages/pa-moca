<p>Pick it up, Bin it!</p>
<p>Here at MoCa, we've started a movement to nudge every resident to take
part in the cleanliness of our estate.</p>

our <a href="https://www.facebook.com/OurGreenMoCa/?__cft__[0]=AZV-pImLTiBC24GYIrrMR8A9-4icHMmGoiP-OSkFzZRPAaQ7P3D08eEhXRYqu5W_jOGAH4JFcub7QhzmmvbsXO7kp_Ali8bTXgJgaSZawylnPVG2iDhvaGeH-suQ7DGCMvnrEiktvE71T72ciZNxfKV8noYmRzjo854ZnQuvXiPsMg&amp;__tn__=kK-R" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Our Green MoCa</a> or
