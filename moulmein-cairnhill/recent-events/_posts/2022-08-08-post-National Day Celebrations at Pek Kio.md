<p>Amazing atmosphere this morning at our National Day Celebrations at Pek
Kio! A sea of red and white, young and old celebrating our special day
with incredible energy! We took time to honour and thank our many frontliners
from our neighbouring Farrer Park Hospital, KK Women's and Children's Hospital,
Tan Tock Seng Hospital and our Tanjong Pagar Town Council workers including
Hassan, Amburumar, Imam, Pandi and Amir.</p>
