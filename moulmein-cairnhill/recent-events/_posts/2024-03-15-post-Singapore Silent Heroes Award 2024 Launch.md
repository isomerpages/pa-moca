<p>Launched the 11th Singapore Silent Heroes (SSH) Awards 2024 this morning
at Pek Kio Community Centre!</p>
<p>Organised by Civilians Association - Singapore, the award recognises ordinary,
everyday people for their extraordinary humanity and compassion towards
people, causes, and missions.</p>
<p>Thanks to SSH Alumni led by Mr Owen Tan (Chairman, SSH Alumni Committee)
for working with our MoCa team to plan a morning of fun activities and
groceries distribution for our seniors. We also had 21 nurses from National
Heart Centre host the game booths for our seniors.</p>
<p>The real heroes in our lives don't wear capes, but their actions inspire
us to be better. We all know them - Ordinary Humans with Extraordinary
Humanity who inspire us with their kindness, strength, and dedication to
others.</p>
