<p>We partner Cyan condo for our Halloween St party, but today's the first
time we went door to door to visit residents, send our CNY greetings and
get feedback on the ground on how we can improve the surrounding environment.</p>
<p>Met old friends like Becks and Alex, and many new friends too along the
two blocks we visited on a cool and rainy Saturday.</p>
