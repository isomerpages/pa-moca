<p>Joined our Cambridge Neighbourhood and Ling Ci Xing Gong (灵慈行宫) for their
Lunar 7th Month Dinner tonight.</p>
<p>灵慈行宫 was built here in 1962, when Cambridge was still a kampong. Daniel,
the chair of 灵慈行宫 told me his grandfather built it and welcomed all members
of the community to celebrate.</p>
<p>灵慈行宫 has been a key member of our local Pek Kio community, and supported
many of our community events and projects through the years.</p>
