<p>The project has encouraged the private estate and heartland estates to
come together and get involved in green initiatives and take ownership
of their neighbourhood's environment. Through the project's community-led
approach, residents have come together to create a space that reflects
their needs and values. It has also sparked conversations around sustainability
and the importance of taking action to tackle climate change at a local
level.</p>
