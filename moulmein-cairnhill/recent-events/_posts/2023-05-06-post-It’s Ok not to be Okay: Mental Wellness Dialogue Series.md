<p>Our event aimed to break down the stigma surrounding mental health and
promote open and honest conversations about our mental wellbeing. Through
a range of activities and talks, we offered support and resources for individuals
who may be facing challenges with their mental health, while also emphasizing
that it's okay to not always feel okay.</p>
<p>We are grateful for the opportunity to raise awareness on such an important
topic and hope to continue the conversation and provide resources for those
in need.</p>
<p>Moulmein - Cairnhill Well Being Circle is training more volunteers to
be peer supporters to identify early signs of mental health issues and
support our community.</p>
