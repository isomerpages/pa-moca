<p>Masjid Ba'alwi is a well known treasure in MoCa - Moulmein-Cairnhill.
I come every year during Ramadan to break fast with the community. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Imam Syed Hassan Mohamed Al-Attas, better known as Habib Hassan, has been
looking after the mosque for over forty years. His late father, Habib Syed
Muhammad Syed Salim Al-Attas, started the mosque in Lewis Road in 1952.</p>
<p>Habib Hassan is also a beloved interfaith leader recognised for his work
building racial and religious harmony in Singapore.</p>
<p>Tonight I brought my MOCA Harmony Circle and Tanglin Neighbourhood Committee
residents to visit and explore the mosque and its many historical artifacts.
Good to also catch up with my Cikgu Yatiman Yusoff and Hawazi Daipi, who
both served our nation as political office holders and MPs.</p>
