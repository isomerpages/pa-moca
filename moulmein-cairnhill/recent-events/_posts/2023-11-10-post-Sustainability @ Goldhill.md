<p>Started our day bright and early at our Goldhill NC's Sustainability @
Goldhill event.</p>
<p>Our Goldhill community has green thumbs. They love to recycle, reuse and
grow things sustainably. They rescue trees and plants, use composting to
grow crops and are learning about greening and gardening every day.</p>
<p>If you live in and around our Goldhill area, join Amy and her team!</p>
