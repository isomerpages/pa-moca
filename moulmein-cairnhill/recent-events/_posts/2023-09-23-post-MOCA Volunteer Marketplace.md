<p>Our MoCa community has so much to offer within and outside our constituency.
That’s why we put together our first Volunteer Marketplace - to showcase
the many volunteer opportunities for our MOCA residents to offer their
time and talents to make an impact!</p>
<p>Ping us at <a href="mailto:Hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Hello@moca.sg</a> to
learn more about the many volunteer opportunities we have on offer!</p>
<p>With today’s successful launch, we’ll be organizing more volunteer marketplaces
in future! Watch this space!</p>
