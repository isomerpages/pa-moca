<p>We look after our seniors here in Pek Kio. Every Chinese New Year, , together
with our Active Aging Centre at Chong Hua Tong, we organize a CNY Lunch
for our seniors here. We also (as always) thank Our Tanjong Pagar Town
Council Team for their hard work.</p>
