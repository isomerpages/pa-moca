regular functional health screenings for them with our partners from Agency
for Integrated Care - AIC Singapore and Silver Generation Office. Our Digital
Ambassadors also help coach our seniors on how to be safe digitally.</p>
<p>Thanks also to our MOCA Youth Network for organizing!</p>
