<p>What a spooktacular Halloween Street Fiesta!!</p>
<p>The whole trail of Ewe Boon Road was lit up with amazing decorations and
residents of all ages added to the fiesta vibe by gamely dressing up in
their best Halloween costumes!</p>
<p>Big shout out to the Resident Volunteers who set up the 8 game booths
along the trail!</p>
