<p>I always enjoy visiting Angullia Mosque. We've worked closely over the
years and have built strong bonds of friendships. Through the COVID challenges
and managing migrant worker flows during long weekends, Haji Gamal and
his team are now family.</p>
<p>So it's always a treat to break fast together with him, the mosque community
our MoCa Harmony Circles, and Tekka Residents' Committee last night.</p>
<p>Ramadan Mubarak to our Angullia Mosque family</p>
