<p>We unveiled Pek Kio Park this afternoon! It will be a green space for
our residents to exercise, plant an edible garden and organize fun activities.</p>
<p>Mayor Denise Phua and I hosted a launch party for our residents living
around the site, at Blk 40 Cambridge Rd this afternoon. Thank you all for
coming, and for your questions and suggestions.</p>
<p>We aim to complete construction of Pek Kio Park by the end of the year
for everyone to enjoy it. A big thanks to NParks and Centre for Liveable
Cities for working so closely with Our Green MoCa to design a park from
the ground up with inputs from our local community.</p>
<p>We cannot wait to see it come to life!</p>
