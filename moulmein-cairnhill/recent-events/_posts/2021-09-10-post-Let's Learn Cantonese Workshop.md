<p>Our Cairnhill CC WEC and Momen jointly organised a Let's Learn Cantonese
workshop sharing some day-to-day Cantonese interaction words.</p>
<p>Our Adviser, Made a surprise visit to close the session. Great thanks
to our speaker Mdm Lee, our organiser Angeline Kwong and Grace Ong and
team and participants who took time off to join us.</p>
