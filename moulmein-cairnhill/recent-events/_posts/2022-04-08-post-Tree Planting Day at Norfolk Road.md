<p>Many young families and MoCa residents took up the green challenge and
planted trees along Norfolk Road yesterday as part of the #SGGreenPlan&amp;#OneMillionTrees
movement. Thanks to NParks for facilitating the experience and our supportive
Adviser, Alvin Tan, who spared no effort and joined the laborious but meaningful
task of tree planting so our community can enjoy the benefits of nature
for generations to come. Drop us a message at <a href="http://hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a> if you'd like to play an active
role in greening our neighbourhood!</p>
