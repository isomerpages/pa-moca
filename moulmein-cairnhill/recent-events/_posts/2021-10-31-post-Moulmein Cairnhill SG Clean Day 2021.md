<p>Our Green MoCa Team got together to pick litter in and around our community
to #KeepSGClean.</p>
<p>A few of us brought our children, to impress upon them the hard work that
our Tanjong Pagar Town Council cleaners do every day, and how every single
one of us have to play a part to keep our environment clean.</p>
