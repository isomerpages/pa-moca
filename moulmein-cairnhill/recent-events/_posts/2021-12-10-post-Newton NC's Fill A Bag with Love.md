<p>BIG thank you to all residents and donors for all their generosity! Over
the span of 2 days, 495 new school bags filled with love are donated to
our beneficiaries.</p>
<p>A special shout-out to Cairnhill Community Club Management Committee for
donating 130 bags to this meaningful cause! Thank you to everyone who has
made this Christmas event possible! We look forward to seeing you at our
future events!</p>
