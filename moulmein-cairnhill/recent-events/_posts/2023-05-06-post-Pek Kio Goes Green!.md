<p>Our Green MoCa invited partners to setup various recycling booths where
residents can drop off their recyclables. The booths were designed to educate
residents about the importance of recycling and how they can reduce waste.
Additionally, the team collaborated with schools in the division to paint
wall murals that educate the community about climate change and the importance
of protecting the environment.</p>
