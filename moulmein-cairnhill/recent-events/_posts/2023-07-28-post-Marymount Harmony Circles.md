<p>Up early this morning to join our Marymount Racial &amp; Religious Harmony
Circle Ultimate Frisbee Tournament held at Eunoia Junior College! This
is the inaugural youth-centric sporting event organised by Marymount Harmony
Circle.</p>
<p>200 youths from Marymount Harmony Circle, Oasis Ultimate, Eunoia Junior
College and other Institutes of Higher Learning (IHLs) came together to
pit their frisbee skills!</p>
<p>Great job Marymount Harmony Circle and Ultimate Oasis Club, on this fantastic
Tournament. Energetic way to cap off Racial and Religious Harmony month
in July!</p>
