<p>Happy New Year to our Viva community! Our first large scale CNY celebration
since the COVID-19 pandemic. Everyone was in high spirits! Great to see
so many happy and familiar faces. We also honoured and thanked our migrant
worker friends who are working on the North South Corridor next to the
condo.</p>
