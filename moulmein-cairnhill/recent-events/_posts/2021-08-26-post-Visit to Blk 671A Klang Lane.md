<p>Grassroots Adviser Mr Alvin Tan and Tekka RC GRLs visited Blk 671A Klang
Lane to check in on our residents if they have been vaccinated and staying
safe.</p>
<p>We were pleased that everyone we had asked were vaccinated and they were
supportive of the often painful decisions the government had to take over
the past month to protect lives while we ramp up our vaccination.</p>
