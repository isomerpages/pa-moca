<p>Easter is typically associated with bunnies and eggs, but it’s really
about love and resurrection.</p>
<p>So it’s touching to see our young and young-at-heart come together to
enjoy one another’s company, in one of our first larger scale physical
community events since we relaxed our COVID measures.</p>
