<p>A picture of our racial and religious harmony in Singapore.</p>
<p>We joined our Hindu friends at Sri Srinivasa Perumal Temple today to observe
the Hindu month of Puratasi.</p>
<p>Puratasi is a special month in the Tamil calendar dedicated to to Lord
Venkateswara, a form of Lord Vishnu, who Hindus believed descended to Earth
in this month to bless his devotees and protect them from the evils of
the Kali Yuga.</p>
<p>During the month of Puratasi, Sri Srinivasa Perumal Temple serves devotees
meals, and blesses our migrant worker community.</p>
<p>It was a wonderful opportunity for us to learn more about Puratasi</p>
