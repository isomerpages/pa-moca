<p>Everyone at our Rangoli Creation for Deepavali @Goldhill Ave Playground
had a blast creating their mini rangolis!</p>
<p>Some of them also had a go at creating floor rangolis under the guidance
of Madam Vijayalakshmi Mahaganapathy.</p>
<p>Some of the mini rangolis created at this event will be on display at
the pavilion of Goldhill Ave playground from 24 to 27 Oct.</p>
<p>Do drop by the pavilion to view these beautiful creations that bring good
luck and prosperity!</p>
<p>To all our friends celebrating the Festival of Lights, we wish you Happy
Deepavali!</p>
