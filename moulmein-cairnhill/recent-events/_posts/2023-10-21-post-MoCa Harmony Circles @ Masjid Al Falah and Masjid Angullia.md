<p>Singaporeans are deeply concerned about the situation in Israel and Gaza.
Many have raised questions, expressed concerns and would like to do something
to help.</p>
<p>Our government has issued statements about the situation, and parliament
will discuss this in detail next month.</p>
<p>On the ground, our MoCa Racial &amp; Religious Harmony Circles came together
today at Masjid Al-Falah and Masjid Angullia (Mosque) to have a frank discussion
of the issue. Christian, Hindu, Sikh, Muslim leaders in our community in
fellowship, prayer and dialogue together, affirming our commitment to racial
and religious harmony in Singapore.</p>
<p>We also pooled our resources and gave generously to RLA Foundation Singapore’s
Humanitarian Relief for Gaza efforts.</p>

</p>
