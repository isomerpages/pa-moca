<p>Our annual MoCa Art Jam is a hit with our families! Thank you to our MOCA
family for putting this together. The many families who showed up today,
including mine, had a wonderful time!</p>
<p>Our MOCA is a community for all ages! If you live here, or would like
to join us for our many such activities, check our <a href="http://MOCA.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOCA.sg</a>!</p>
