<p>Balmoral NC partnered with National Environment Agency (NEA) and Singapore
Civil Defence Force to organize a Fire Safety and Dengue Awareness outreach
session to the residents of Wing on Life Garden this afternoon.</p>
<p>Residents of all ages gained some valuable tips on putting out fires and
how to prevent dengue breeding at their homes.</p>
