<p>Moulmein-Cairnhill MAECs came together to organise our first sit down
meal for our MoCa community since the start of the pandemic.</p>
<p>Residents of all ages and races came together to break fast together.</p>
<p>A special highlight of the event was the habuan distribution to our Town
Council friends who have worked tirelessly to keep our estate clean.</p>
