<p>It's May Day, or Labour Day. A day for us to recognise and thank our workers.</p>
<p>We do that regularly here in MoCa, with Our Migrant Guests (OMG) initiative.
To identify, recognise and thank the many workers who build our roads,
hospitals, schools, flats. This afternoon, our Goldhill NC residents visited
the workers building our North South Corridor along Whitley Rd to hand
goodie bags and refreshments, and to spend time getting to know them.</p>
<p>We know that the work has caused inconvenience to residents, and we work
closely with Land Transport Authority colleagues to minimise the disruptions.
At the same time, the workers are working hard to complete the project
on time.</p>
<p>I encouraged them to put their own safety above all else. They should
complete their work promptly but not at the expense of their safety. And
I assured them that I will work with residents to seek their support and
understanding.</p>
<p>Thank you all for making this afternoon meaningful. Please ping me if
you'd like to join Our Migrant Guests team.</p>
