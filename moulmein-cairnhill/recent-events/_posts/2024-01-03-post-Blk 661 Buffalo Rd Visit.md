this block more than 3 times over the past 3.5 years as MP.</p>

at the Racecourse Rd Pick Up and Drop Off point, and the many support that
our government is providing, including our latest CDC vouchers.</p>
<p>Thank you Team Tekka for joining me on a most impactful visit tonight.</p>
