<p>Pool, Prata, People!</p>
<p>A fun-filled family Saturday with our Belmond Green community. It's our
first time working with them to pull this together. Belmond Green is one
of the larger condos in MoCa - Moulmein-Cairnhill, which adds to the fun
and festivities!</p>
<p>A big thank you to new MCST Chair Tan Zhi Yang and his team, who worked
tirelessly alongside our ever-incredible Balmoral NC team to get this off
the ground.</p>
<p>So happy to meet so many friends and residents about! Thanks for your
hospitality</p>

<p>We will do more! Watch this space!</p>
