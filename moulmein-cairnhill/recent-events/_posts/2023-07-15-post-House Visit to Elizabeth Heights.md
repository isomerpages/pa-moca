<p>Elizabeth &nbsp;Heights! 40 years old now, it's post modern architectural
design is timeless - and reminds one of a 1980s Star Wars set!</p>
<p>And the Force is strong with its inhabitants, some of whom are old family
friends of mine . And many I met today are new friends who have lived here
for some time.</p>
<p>Thanks Sharona and Team Cairnhill NC for spending your Sunday afternoon
getting us all prepped for National Day!</p>
