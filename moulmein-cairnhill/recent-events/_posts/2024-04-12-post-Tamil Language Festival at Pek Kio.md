<p>I've been privileged to support our annual Tamil Language Festival for
the past few years.</p>
<p>This year's run is themed “Odi Vilaiyaadu Nanba!” (Run and Play, My Friend),
which comprises an amazing race where teams must answer questions related
to Tamil classics and literature across Pek Kio.</p>
<p>We strongly support the preservation of our languages, like Tamil (which
is one of Singapore's official languages). And I'm so encouraged that our
youths are actively finding creative ways to make the mastery of the language
fun and engaging! I spoke briefly in halting Tamil as well</p>
<p>I also encouraged them to make use of Ministry of Culture, Community and
Youth - MCCY's SG Heritage Plans 2.0 to conduct more such activities!</p>
<p>Congrats Pek Kio CC Indian Activities Executive Committee and வளர்தமிழ்
இயக்கம் Tamil Language Council for organizing today's event</p>
