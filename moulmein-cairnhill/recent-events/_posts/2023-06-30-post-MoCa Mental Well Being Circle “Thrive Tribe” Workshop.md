<p>Our MoCa Mental Well-Being Circle is helping train our residents with
basic skills so they can support their peers, families and community. Held
in partnership with Limitless Singapore, we hosted our “Thrive Tribe” workshop
this weekend to “build supportive connections that bind”. The workshop
helps our residents learn how to cope and help other cope with life's challenges,
and support one another as we deal with mental well-being issues.</p>
<p>Our next training is on 15 July here in Cairnhill Community Club. Please
ping our Moulmein - Cairnhill Well Being Circle!</p>
