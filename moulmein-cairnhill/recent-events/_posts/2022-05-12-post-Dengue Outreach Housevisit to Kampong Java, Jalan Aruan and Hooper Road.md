<p>There’s a dengue cluster at Kg Java, Jalan Aruan and the Hooper neighbourhood.
We’ve been working closely with our NEA team to address the situation by
checking households fogging, treating the drains and stagnant water, and
getting our residents on board to minimise mosquito breeding sites. We
are also working with construction sites around the area.</p>
<p>Visited our residents personally tonight to raise awareness, hand out
dengue kits, and check in on potential dengue hotspots. There’s work to
be done.</p>
