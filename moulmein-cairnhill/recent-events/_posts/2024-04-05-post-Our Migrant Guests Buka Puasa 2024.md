<p>At MoCa, we honour, respect and recognise our many migrant workers who
help build our schools, roads, bridges, hospitals, and keep our living
environment clean and working every day! Every Ramadan, we host a Buka
Puasa for them to celebrate and thank them for their contributions.</p>
<p>Caring for them is also a weekly affair. My Our Migrant Guests team of
volunteers look out and after them every week. Please join our efforts!
Ping us at <a href="mailto:hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a>.</p>
