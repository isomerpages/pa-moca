<p>We’ve just started a &nbsp;special project here in MoCa for kids with
special needs who love football!</p>
<p>Thanks to Coach Arun and our MOCA Sports Chair Delvin, we’ve been organizing
training sessions for these amazing kids on Saturdays here at Pek Kio Community
Centre. I was inspired by Coach Arun - who has been coaching disability
football for sometime now, so I asked if we could do something together
with him here in MOCA.</p>
<p>We also spent time with their parents and caregivers, who shared with
me that they are both thankful for the respite and the chance for their
kids to enjoy training and football with a community who cares. The families
come from all over Singapore.</p>
<p>We’re delighted to be able to kick start this project. If you’re keen
to find out more and wish to contribute to our fledgling efforts, please
ping me!</p>
