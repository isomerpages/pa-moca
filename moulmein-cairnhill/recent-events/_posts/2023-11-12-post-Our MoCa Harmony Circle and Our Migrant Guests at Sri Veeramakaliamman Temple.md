<p>Happy Deepavali!</p>
<p>Our MoCa Harmony Circles was up bright and early at Sri Veeramakaliamman
Temple, Singapore to learn about and honour the festivities and practices
of our Hindu community.</p>
<p>Representatives from our mosques, gurdwaras, churches, temples and clans,
and from all walks of life - youths and seniors were present this morning.
They stayed back to distribute goodies to devotees and the many guest workers
who came to the Temple.</p>
<p>This is Singapore’s “secret sauce” - our precious racial and religious
unity that we work hard to preserve across generations.</p>
<p>If you are keen to support our efforts on both the Harmony Circle or Migrant
Guests fronts, please email <a href="mailto:hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a>
</p>
