<p>Our MoCa Harmony Circles is a key anchor in our community, bringing our
different faith and racial groups together to learn from one another and
build lasting bonds.</p>
<p>We gathered yesterday to give our the Ministry of Culture, Community and
Youth - MCCY appointment certificates and discussed ideas for implementation,
including during Racial Harmony Month this month.</p>
<p>We're looking for members to join! Ping us at <a href="mailto:hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a>
