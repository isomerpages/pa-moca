<p>With the mercury on the rise, we're thrilled to share that our sponsor,
PAL Line Pte Ltd, generously responded to our residents' needs! When asked
what they could contribute, we suggested fans – and we're immensely grateful
that they wholeheartedly embraced our idea. Thanks to the Pal Line team's
sponsorship and fan installations, along with the provision of bento meals
and goodies, our residents are now enjoying much-needed relief.</p>
<p>A heartfelt shoutout goes to the amazing volunteers from MoCa Green Repair
Kopitiam, Owen RC, and MoCa CDWF for their valuable support!</p>
