<p>My family and I have been volunteering with Hope Initiative Alliance since
the COVID-19 pandemic, supporting their efforts to serve our Guest Worker
community.</p>
<p>So we were happy to again show up for the Together in Singapore Gala Dinner
organised by Alliance of Guest Workers Outreach and Novena Church Sowers.
We enjoyed the games and performances!</p>
<p>We also thanked our Guest Workers for their hard work and contributions
to build Singapore. ACE Group, Singapore Ministry of Manpower, VWOs and
volunteers are doing what we can to support them.</p>
<p>A big thank you to the partners and donors who made such a meaningful
event possible.</p>
<p>Keen to get involved in supporting our migrant worker friends? Reach out
to HIA and AGWO, and the many activities they have lined up for International
Migrants Month (IMM) happening now!</p>
