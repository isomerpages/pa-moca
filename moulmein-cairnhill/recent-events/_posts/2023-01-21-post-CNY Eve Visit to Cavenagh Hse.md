<p>Visited our residents at Cavenagh House this Chinese New Year Eve!</p>
<p>So nice to see so many families busy preparing for reunion dinners or
taking a break to spend with loved ones!</p>
<p>It’s a cosy neighbourhood with a warm community. Met many new and old
friends there from so many seasons of my life. It’s my second visit in
2.5 years. Gerald, Roger and team have been looking after the estate and
the community well.</p>
