<p>Anglo-Chinese School (Primary) is one of the schools at the heart of our
MoCa division. To celebrate the ACS Family's 138th Founder's Day, Principal
Leong-Ho Hil May, parents and students prepared goodie bags to bless our
Pek Kio seniors this morning.</p>
<p>They hung out with our seniors, chatted and even sang with them. So happy
and proud to join them as MP, and a fellow ACSian!</p>
