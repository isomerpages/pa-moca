<p>We are piloting another rest area for our Migrant Worker friends visiting
Little India on Sundays. Working with Singapore Ministry of Manpower's
ACE team, we set up a large tent on the open field at Birch Rd for a movie
night for them, and a place to rest, dance and relax.</p>
<p>We are partnering many friends and organizations who care for Our Migrant
Guests, including ItsRainingRaincoats, HealthServe, Hope Initiative Alliance,
MoCa, Singapore Indian Chamber of Commerce &amp; Industry and others to
build this initiative.</p>
<p>If you are keen to support this meaningful initiative, please ping me
at <a href="mailto:hello@moca.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a>.</p>
