<p>Amidst the CNY Celebrations, our MoCa community leaders came together
to conduct a Table Top Exercise to prepare themselves for any emergencies
that may impact our community.</p>
<p>Terrorist attacks, riots, misinformation, armed conflict, fires, pandemics.
These can hit us at any time. So even as we celebrate the CNY season, we
must prepare, because when better than during peace time.</p>
<p>Thank you to all our MOCA leaders for making time. Our next crisis can
happen anytime, and we are all in the front line. So proud of each and
every one of you!</p>
