<p>With our Award-Winning Our Green MoCa Team this afternoon to launch our
Clean Pod with Public Hygiene Council and Edible Garden at our Green Pod
at Blk 40 Cambridge Rd!</p>
<p>Our Green MOCA does incredible work to raise awareness of, and execute
programs to reduce waste, recycle and reuse! Please ping them if you’re
keen to be a part of our exciting green movement here in MoCa!</p>
