<p>A full Saturday visiting residents in their homes at Blk 662 Buffalo Rd,
to update them on improvements around the Tekka area and to check in on
their needs.</p>
<p>I shared about our new Tekka Centre renovations, the new pick up point
for private cars at Racecourse Road, Govt support including CDC Vouchers,
S&amp;CC and U Save rebates, and many others.</p>
<p>Also dropped by a wake to pay respects to and say goodbye to a resident.</p>
