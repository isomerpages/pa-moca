<p>We just planted our 1000th Tree in Our Tanjong Pagar, right here in Radin
Mas!</p>
<p>Tanjong Pagar is special because of the legacy that Mr Lee Kuan Yew left
us. My teammates Chan Chun Sing, Indranee Rajah, Eric Chua, Melvin Yong
杨益财, Joan Pereira and I are committed to building on Mr Lee’s legacy of
a green Tanjong Pagar and green Singapore.</p>
