<p>We were greeted by many happy faces at Rich Mansion as we gave out goody
bags to the residents to celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival!</p>
<p>Due to the current restrictions and recent rise in local cases, we regret
that we had to cancel our Mid-Autumn Festival celebration at Rich Mansion.</p>
<p>But we do hope to return soon for future events when the pandemic eases!</p>
<p>Till then, stay safe!</p>
