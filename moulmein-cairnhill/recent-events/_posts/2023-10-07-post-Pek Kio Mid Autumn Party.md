<p>Our Pek Kio family had a most enjoyable evening last night, celebrating
Mid Autumn.</p>
<p>We had games, performances, ice cream, lanterns, quizzes and music! As
per our tradition, I told stories about 嫦娥 后裔 and of 朱元璋, the Red Turban
Rebellion and mooncakes and got the kids to fill in the gaps of the story
and gave prizes!</p>
<p>Thank you Lawrence, Stephanie and Pek Kio Community Centre for the wonderful
event! MoCa - Moulmein-Cairnhill</p>
