<p>Hasan, Veera, Nazir - these are just some of names of our hardworking
Tanjong Pagar Town Council staff keeping our neighbourhood in tip top condition.</p>
<p>Every Deepavali, we get to celebrate the Festival of Lights with them
in a small ceremony here at Pek Kio Community Centre. We hope to do something
a little larger scale when the situation permits.</p>
<p>Thank you Kabilan and Tanglin Community Club IAEC for pulling the celebratory
package for our guys.</p>
