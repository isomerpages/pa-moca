<p>Join us on 14 Sep 2024, Saturday, at Blk 50 Dorset Road Multi-Purpose
Hall from 7:00pm - 10:00pm for an evening of fun and festivity!</p>
<p>Enjoy tea appreciation, test your knowledge with a lantern quiz, unleash
your creativity with DIY Chinese fan activities, and indulge in delicious
treats at our food booths. Don’t miss the movie screening of “Puss in Boots:
The Last Wish” at 7:00pm!</p>
<p>Bring your own mats &amp; water bottles, and stand a chance to win amazing
lucky draw prizes like a Prism+ TV, Apple Watch, Nintendo Switch, and more!</p>
