<p>Our Mid-Autumn Festival 2024, which was overwhelmingly well-received in
2022, is back! It will be held on 14th September, Saturday, 4.30-7.30pm,
at Thomson Road Playground.</p>
<p>Expect lots of fun, food, and activities booths this round for your entire
family. The Mid-Autumn lantern procession will also be held at 7pm</p>
