<p>Join Us for a Fun-Filled Day on the Water!
<br>
<br>Join us on Saturday, November 30th for Kayak, Clean &amp; Explore — a
unique opportunity to enjoy our beautiful waterways while helping keep
them clean! Paddle alongside friends and neighbors, and let’s work together
to protect our environment, one stroke at a time!
<br>
<br>Where: Singapore Canoe Federation
<br>When: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
<br>Bus Pickup: 8:15 AM at Cairnhill CC
<br>
<br>Open to all 12 years and above (just $5 per person, two per kayak!). All
necessary items will be provided, but don’t forget to bring a water bottle,
sunblock, a towel, and closed shoes!
<br>
<br>Scan the QR code to register or visit <strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/ogmkayakcleanexplore2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/ogmkayakcleanexplore2024</a></strong> and
let’s make waves for a cleaner, greener future!</p>
