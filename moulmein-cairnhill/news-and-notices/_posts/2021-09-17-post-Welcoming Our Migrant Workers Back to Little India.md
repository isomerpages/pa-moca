<p>Delighted to welcome our guest workers to Little India this morning!</p>
<p>Our Little India Tekka (LIT) Taskforce is working with Ministry of Manpower
to welcome migrant workers back to the community, in a pilot run by MOM's
ACE (Assurance, Care &amp; Engagement) group to allow workers living in
dorms to return to the community after more than 17 months.</p>
<p>And our Little India is special to many of them, with their places of
worship, shopping and food. This is a small start and I would like to do
more.</p>
