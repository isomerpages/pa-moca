<p>Contractor, Penta-Bachy Joint Venture (PBJV) is scheduled to conduct preparatory
works along Thomson Road from&nbsp;<strong>15 December to 22 December 2023</strong>,
in advance of a traffic diversion set to occur from&nbsp;<strong>8:00pm on 23 December 2023 to 5:30am on 24 December 2023</strong>.
Concurrently, the bus stop (No. 50041) located in front of Novena Lodge
will be relocated on&nbsp;<strong>24 December 2023</strong>.</p>
