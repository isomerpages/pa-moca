<p>Dengue cases in our Robin / Balmoral / Stevens estate are rising. The
Moulmein-Cairnhill Team has been engaging our residents to update them
on National Environment Agency (NEA) actions to remove mosquito breeding
sources through intensive indoor and outdoor search-and-destroy operations,
and applying larvicide and fogging where necessary.</p>
<p>Everyone needs to play their part. Let's stay vigilant and fight dengue
together!</p>
