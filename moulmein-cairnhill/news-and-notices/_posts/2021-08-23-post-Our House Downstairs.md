<p>What does art have to do with mental heath? As research shows, a lot!&nbsp;</p>
<p>The pandemic has taken a toll on our seniors’ mental wellbeing.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our&nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/mocaminds/?__cft__[0]=AZWrZ5MaLQWh5i6ZPGp83moeuuWbo3sHRDq9egpkWeGe_nwn-yYLrh3F_xziiOl_vGfziv9vgQUwTOmiOhhva4KQQ6yG_jTw5-RdJwHb3-p7Xbnq0t3kQ6iZLva6KCcBi38x_W-5Q3nTTF51Z97BHoeG3Xmbz0g_c-evil7IK0y5rA&amp;__tn__=kK-R" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Moulmein - Cairnhill Mental Health Task Force</a>&nbsp;(or
MoCa Minds), in partnership with&nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/thirtytwocmsg/?__cft__[0]=AZWrZ5MaLQWh5i6ZPGp83moeuuWbo3sHRDq9egpkWeGe_nwn-yYLrh3F_xziiOl_vGfziv9vgQUwTOmiOhhva4KQQ6yG_jTw5-RdJwHb3-p7Xbnq0t3kQ6iZLva6KCcBi38x_W-5Q3nTTF51Z97BHoeG3Xmbz0g_c-evil7IK0y5rA&amp;__tn__=kK-R" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">thirtytwocm</a>,
has launched 'Our House Downstairs' weekly art jamming sessions on Wednesdays
to look after our seniors’ mental wellbeing.</p>
<p>We've created a safe space for our seniors to drop by, make new friends
or connect with old ones, keep their minds active, engaged and creative
through art sessions.</p>
<p>Know someone who can benefit from this? Ping us at&nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/mocaminds/?__cft__[0]=AZWrZ5MaLQWh5i6ZPGp83moeuuWbo3sHRDq9egpkWeGe_nwn-yYLrh3F_xziiOl_vGfziv9vgQUwTOmiOhhva4KQQ6yG_jTw5-RdJwHb3-p7Xbnq0t3kQ6iZLva6KCcBi38x_W-5Q3nTTF51Z97BHoeG3Xmbz0g_c-evil7IK0y5rA&amp;amp;__tn__=kK-R" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">hello@moca.sg</a>&nbsp;and
we'll update as soon as there are available slots!</p>
