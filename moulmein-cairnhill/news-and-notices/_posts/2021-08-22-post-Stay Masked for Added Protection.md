<p>From 26 August to 26 September 2021, every Singapore household will be
eligible to collect 50 medical grade surgical masks and 25 N95 masks at
select CapitaLand malls and supermarket outlets.&nbsp;</p>
<p>What you need: your SP residential bill and a reusable bag to carry the
free masks.</p>
<p>Not all stores are collection points. Please check&nbsp;<a href="https://stayprepared.sg/masks?fbclid=IwAR1XVVCZ6KEGu-dLQlhMli-OemwYqExDok-Knr-wSdBlg3e5U1gzXoe7-pQ" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://stayprepared.sg/masks</a>&nbsp;for
your nearest collection point, opening hours and stock level before collecting.&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>Do not rush. There is one month to collect and sufficient stock.</em>
