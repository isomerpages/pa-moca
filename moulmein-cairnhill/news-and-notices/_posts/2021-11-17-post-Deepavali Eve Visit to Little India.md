<p>Our Grassroots Adviser, Mr Alvin Tan headed doen to Little India on Deepavali
Eve to check in on our LiSHA (Little India Shopkeepers and Heritage Association)
community, and Singapore Tourism Board and Singapore Police Force colleagues.</p>
<p>COVID-19 has continued to be hard on our local businesses and our officers
on the ground are making sure that folks can go about their Deepavali preparations
safely amidst the pandemic.</p>
<p>Thank you Ruthra Kannan and team for your kind support and understanding
as we get through this together.</p>
