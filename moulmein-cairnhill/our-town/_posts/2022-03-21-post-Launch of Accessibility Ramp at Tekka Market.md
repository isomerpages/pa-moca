<p>We thank, honour and remember the hands that build the infrastructure
around us. So thank you Shohel, Biplop and Venkat for your hard work in
building this accessibility ramp at Tekka Market.</p>
<p>The ramp will benefit scores of stall keepers, customers and residents,
especially our seniors, persons with disabilities and caregivers with strollers.
The work took a while to complete due to COVID delays. But we're all so
thankful it is finally completed.</p>
<p>You'll see their hand prints on the banner we put up next to the ramp
