<p>LTA will be carrying out a road diversion along Kampong Road and Keng
Lee Road from<strong> 23 February 2023, Thursday 2100 hrs to 24 February 2023, Friday 0500 hrs.</strong> Part
of the works would involve in removal of bridge works and installation
of steel decking. The installation of the steel decking will be carried
out progressively after the closure of bridge</p>
