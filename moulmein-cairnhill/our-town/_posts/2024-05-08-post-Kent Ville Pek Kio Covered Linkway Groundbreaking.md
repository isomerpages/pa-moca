<p>We have broken ground on our Covered Linkway linking Kent Ville and Pek
Kio Market.</p>
<p>It's a mini landmark project for our residents. MP Denise Phua, myself
and Tanjong Pagar and Jalan Besar Town Council teams worked hard behind
the scenes with LTA to get this done</p>
<p>Thank you to the many teams for making this happen! We're looking forward
to completing the works by 3rd quarter of this year!</p>
