<p>Dear Residents,</p>
<p>We opened an interim zebra crossing across Handy Road this morning. We
were joined by residents from 8@Mount Sophia and Orchard Suites Residences
for the mini opening.</p>
<p>The crossing will help pedestrians, especially parents with prams or young
children cross Handy Rd more safely. We are working on a permanent signalised
crossing in Nov 2022, to align with upcoming developments in the area.
We will share more details in due time.</p>
<p>Thank you for your feedback and suggestions, and also to Team for working
hard with our team to make this happen.</p>
